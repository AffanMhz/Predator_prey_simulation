Lotka-Volterra (LV):
dR/dt = α R − β R F
dF/dt = δ R F − γ F
Parameter mapping (discrete → continuous):
If p is probability of reproduction per timestep (small), and Δt = 1/fps,
α ≈ ln(1 + p) / Δt ≈ p / Δt for small p.
(similarly for other rates)
